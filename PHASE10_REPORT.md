## Architecture: Three New Layers

```
L24 ── Knowledge Evolution Layer
│         • Knowledge mutates and evolves autonomously
│         • Multiple discovery ecologies evolve independently
│         • Fitness-based selection for knowledge structures
│
L25 ── Civilization Governance Layer
│         • Stability and alignment monitoring
│         • Automatic intervention on detected risks
│         • Overall system health tracking
│
L26 ── Singularity Coordination Layer
│         • Civilization-wide metric tracking
│         • Resource optimization across all systems
│         • Self-sustaining operation verification
```

Total system: **26 layers** (L-2 through L26)

---

## Components

### P10.1 / L24 Knowledge Evolution Engine
- Knowledge mutates through refinement, combination, abstraction, and specialization
- Fitness-based selection preserves valuable knowledge structures
- Continuous evolution without human intervention

### P10.2 Recursive Discovery Ecosystem
- Discoverers create better discoverers, recursively
- Higher-depth discoverers exhibit superior performance
- Self-improvement through performance feedback loops

### P10.3 Universal Knowledge Fabric 2.0
- Integrates 8 domains: science, math, engineering, technology, economics, medicine, governance, education
- Single evolving structure with cross-domain connections
- Integration score tracks domain cohesion

### P10.4 Discovery Ecology
- Four independent research ecologies: Conservative, Radical, Exploratory, Verification
- Each evolves with its own productivity and stability dynamics
- Ecologies maintain distinct approaches to discovery

### P10.5 Meta-Knowledge Civilization
- Studies fundamental questions: What is knowledge? How does it evolve? How do discoveries emerge?
- Generates hypotheses and tests them against evidence
- Confidence-weighted knowledge about knowledge itself

### P10.6 Civilization Memory
- Stores all artifacts: theories, experiments, discoveries, failures, paradigm shifts, institutions
- Importance-weighted retention for indefinite preservation
- Comprehensive record of civilization-scale knowledge

### P10.7 / L25 Civilization Governance Layer
- Real-time stability and alignment monitoring
- Automatic risk detection and intervention
- Targets: 0.95 stability, 0.98 alignment

### P10.8 Discovery Forecasting Engine
- Predicts future discoveries, technologies, bottlenecks, and paradigm shifts
- Domain-specific predictions with probability estimates
- Accuracy tracking over time

### P10.9 Universal Problem Network
- Connects all problems into a single graph: energy, climate, materials, economics, policy, society
- Cross-connection enables system-level solution discovery
- Tracks proposed and implemented solutions per problem

### P10.10 / L26 Self-Sustaining Civilization
- Four key metrics: discovery_rate, knowledge_growth, governance_stability, coordination_efficiency
- Continuous improvement toward targets
- Verification of self-sustaining operation

---

## Key Design Decisions

1. **Phase 10 is the final roadmap phase** — All 26 layers and 10 phases are implemented. The system moves from "theory creator" (Phase 1) to "self-sustaining knowledge civilization" (Phase 10).

2. **Knowledge evolves itself** — P10.1 and P10.4 eliminate the need for externally designed research workflows. Knowledge mutates, ecologies compete, and the fittest structures survive.

3. **Recursive discovery** — P10.2 implements the "agents create discoverers who create better discoverers" paradigm, enabling open-ended capability growth.

4. **Problems are connected** — P10.9 ensures energy, climate, materials, economics, policy, and society are not siloed but fully interconnected, enabling cross-domain solutions.

5. **Governance at every level** — L25 governance operates alongside L23 superintelligence governance. Safety and alignment are not bolted on but architected in.
